The Ninja Remote is a free, fun and easy-to-use app that turns your iPhone into a remote control for your Ninja Blocks connected devices. You can control your Ninja compatible devices from anywhere in the world, all with the flick of a finger.

The Ninja Remote app has been designed to let you speedily turn on and off devices, control the color of your lights and more.

Checkout the Ninja Blocks community at http://forums.ninjablocks.com .
